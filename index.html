Class Spotlight

Classic Barre.

M·Power Classic Barre combines the stretching and toning of yoga, the core work of Pilates, and the strengthening principles of Ballet. A low impact class that uses props ranging from hand weights to stability balls to add challenge, Classic Barre is sure to tone your muscles and firm your body! Move mindfully, all the while building coordination, strength, and self-awareness. This class is an all levels class, beginners are always welcome.
